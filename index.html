Developing Autoencoder: Incremental Bottleneck Expansion Leads to an Informed Latent Space
David Vogenauer, Deyue Kong, Jonas Elpelt, Markos Genios, Matthias Kaschube
AAAI 2026 NeuroAI Workshop

This paper introduces the Developing Autoencoder (Dev-AE), a biologically inspired model that incrementally expands its latent space during training, which we found fosters ordered, sparse, and more diverse representations.
